About Karnataka

Karnataka is one of India's most diverse tourism destinations offering royal palaces, UNESCO heritage sites, hill stations, beaches and wildlife sanctuaries. From Bengaluru’s innovation to Hampi’s ancient ruins, the state blends tradition with technology.

History

Karnataka was ruled by powerful dynasties like the Chalukyas, Hoysalas and the Vijayanagara Empire, leaving behind architectural masterpieces.

Culture

The state is famous for Mysore Dasara, Yakshagana, Carnatic music, sandalwood crafts and silk sarees.

Climate

Coastal Karnataka is tropical, the plateau region is moderate, and hill stations like Coorg remain cool throughout the year.

Top Tourist Attractions

Mysore Palace

A royal residence known for its grand architecture and world-famous Dasara celebrations.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring stunning temple ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Coorg

A scenic hill station with coffee estates, waterfalls and breathtaking landscapes.
